Our Mission

LifeStream has been at the forefront of blood donation for over 5 years. We provide high-quality blood testing and storage, ensuring that every drop counts. Our facility maintains the highest standards of hygiene and efficiency.

We handle all major blood groups including A+, B+, AB+, and O (both positive and negative). Our quality control team ensures that the quantity and quality of blood meet medical requirements for surgeries, emergencies, and chronic treatments.
